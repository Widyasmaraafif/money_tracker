Privacy Policy for Money Tracker

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Money Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

a. Personal Data

The App is designed to work offline and primarily stores data locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the App.

b. Financial Data

All transaction data, budgets, and financial records you enter into the App are stored locally on your device using Hive storage. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers.

c. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

2. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, all data stored within the App will be permanently removed unless you have performed a manual backup (if applicable).

3. Permissions

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
