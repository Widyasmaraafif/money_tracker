# Privacy Policy for Money Tracker

**Effective Date: March 18, 2026**

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### a. Personal Data
The App is designed to work offline and primarily stores data locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the App.

All transaction data, budgets, and financial records you enter into the App are stored locally on your device using Hive storage. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers.
